I get it comics are considered harder to follow than Manga. Yes there are multiple continuties, yes there are multiple earths and versions of the same character, but getting into comics isn't that hard to do really. All you gotta do is start reading one run and go from there. Hell my first Nightwing book was an issue from Tom Taylor's NW run(Yikes i know, Although It wasn't my introduction to him just my first physical comic). Anyways, So you want to start following Dick Grayson and all his journeys through the DC comic books? Here is my reccomended reading list.

I know most people wont be able to get or obtain the golden age comics or much of the older comics without selling an arm and a leg so My list will provide comics you would be able to Find in comic shops still or you can decently obtain it somewhere(Or if it's likely to be acessed on Totally legit Comic sites :>) This isn't an indepth reading list but just something where you can get started on Dick Grayson and not get overwhelmed with his 1 million comic runs lol
